Rewired Imagination is an online ideation platform, launched in July 2013, from Rewired State, open to everyone, to respond to challenges made by people with ideas, who want to test the waters or find out more before prototyping starts.

The main emphasis is based around ideas, community, the idea of experts, and knowledgeable judges reviewing submissions made.

If you want to see what others think of your idea, see what's Imaginable, by others.
