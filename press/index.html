About

Rewired Imagination is a product from Rewired State, built on the Rewired Reality functionality.

Standard description

Rewired Imagination is an online ideation platform allowing ideas to be generated, critiqued, peer-reviewed, and ultimately judged, by anyone able to access the website. Competition is integral to the way it's been designed.

We understand the importance of domain knowledge experts, as well as challenge-raisers wanting to score submissions against given criteria, providing tools for both.

To take things forward, there's an easy method to take the brief, and winning entries forward via Rewired Reality, or into a Rewired State hack day.
